What do we do?

Awareness is the greatest agent of change. We are on a mission to further that. We use technology to empower citizens through health education.

The role of Health care professionals should go beyond delivery of medical/ dental care and should extend to include basic education about important health related conditions. This information should be clearly presented and effectively communicated to persuade their patients to change their behaviour. To this end, the app provides an effective patient education aid that can be used in hospitals and clinics to improve awareness about OSMF and areca nut usage. It can facilitate a person or a community to reflect upon their risk behaviours and change them to reduce their risk and vulnerability.

Knowledge about health conditions improves health seeking behaviour. Therefore, educating communities on Areca nut usage and the effects of OSMF and building awareness of the extent of the problem by connecting with the target population is a huge part of what we do. The app looks to engage its audience to improve their knowledge on the diagnosis, symptoms as well as their treatment options at each stage of the disease. However, we also recognise that awareness has to be translated into an effective behaviour change to have an impact on the problem.
